Prayagraj

संगम नगरी

The Heart of U.P

Prayagraj, also known as Allahabad, is a famous pilgrimage city in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh. It is famous for the holy Triveni Sangam where the three rivers: the Ganges, the Yamuna and the Saraswati meet at the same point and become one.
